Chocolate Moose

Description

This is reserved for VERY rare events because it requires a lot of work but looks amazing!

Ingredients

  • 1 moose
  • 40 lbs Hershey chocolate
  • 17 containers Cool Whip
  • 1 cherry

Steps

  1. To capture a moose, send your spouse to Alaska, or have one delivered via UPS.
  2. In meantime, melt the chocolate in very large double boiler
  3. Keep warm
  4. Tie up moose with rope
  5. Using the moose's tail as support, slowly dunk it in molten chocolate, coating it entirely.
  6. Place moose artistically on a large tray, then place in the refrigerator for two days to help the chocolate harden.
  7. Put some Cool Whip on the chocolate moose's garnish and add a cherry on top.
  8. Serve immediately.
