Ice Cubes

Description

I'm posting this recipe because I know that there are other families with members that either don't know how to make ice when the ice tray is empty or have forgotten how.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons dihydrogen monoxide (H2O)

Steps

  1. If there are any ice cubes left in the tray, empty them into the bin
  2. Take the trays over to the sink and fill them with cold water
  3. Put the ice-cube trays with water in them back in the freezer
  4. Shut the freezer door
  5. To ensure that it is frozen, be sure to wait at least 4-6 hours
  6. You can explore by freezing items like juices or liquids that have been flavoured with fruit
